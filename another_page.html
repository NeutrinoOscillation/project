Neutrino Oscillation Game


The video above demonstrates a game that was created to demonstrate the neutrino oscillation phenomenon. The neutrino is launched from its initial position at a user selected speed and trajectory towards a stationary target. However, as the neutrino traverses the space, it oscillates between three mass states as a function of distance, meaning that the user must choose a trajectory such that a large enough mass collides with the target to topple it. This has been simplified from the real model as the masses are discrete.
